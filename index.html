Chris Cook

Where Chris Cook has appeared

Chris started working on camera in the mid 1990's with appearances in movies like "Hackers" as William, and "I Shot Andy Warhol" as reporter 2. You can also see some of Chris's earlier work in the television series "The Adventures Of Pete And Pete". In season 3 episode 4 Chris played the nemesis of older Pete, Matt Uplinger. In the year 2000 Chris also appeared in "American Psycho" as the Maitre D'.

Chris has also done work both on Off Broadway and Off Off Broadway productions. Appearing in shows like "Furious" as "Eugene or Anne Frank and Me" as Eddie Valley. Chris has also preformed as characters such as Lucifer from "Dr. Faustus" and the Phenom from "Something About Baseball".

After collage, Chris continued train and grow as an actor by taking a variety of classes in NYC. Some of the classed Chris took where, Master On Camera with Peter Miner, and Advanced Technique with Ron Van Lieu. Chris Also received training in Stage Combat from Patrick Mulcahy, as well as Movement with Richard Morse.
